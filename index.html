Your SLO skill statement

Skill Profile · Grade 3 Math

Performance-level descriptors

Each level's score range is set by your percentage bands. Tap any descriptor to customize the wording for your class — this becomes the shared language you'll use to place and grow students.

Well Above Typical
Student independently and consistently solves one- and two-step multiplication and division word problems with a high level of accuracy and efficiency. Student flexibly applies multiple strategies, models, and equations to solve complex or unfamiliar problems and communicates mathematical reasoning using precise mathematical vocabulary and detailed explanations.
Above Typical
Student accurately solves one- and two-step multiplication and division word problems using appropriate strategies, models, and equations. Student explains reasoning clearly, demonstrates strong conceptual understanding, and applies skills independently in most grade-level situations.
Typical
Student solves grade-level multiplication and division word problems using strategies, models, or equations with occasional teacher support. Student demonstrates developing understanding of mathematical reasoning and can explain thinking, though accuracy or consistency may vary.
Below Typical
Student demonstrates partial understanding of multiplication and division concepts and requires frequent support to solve word problems. Student may struggle to select effective strategies, represent problems with models, or clearly explain mathematical reasoning.
Well Below Typical
Student demonstrates limited understanding of multiplication and division problem-solving concepts and is unable to consistently solve grade-level word problems, even with significant teacher support. Student has difficulty using models, strategies, equations, and mathematical language to explain thinking.
