Skill Profile · Grade 3 Math

Performance-level descriptors

Each level's score range is set by your percentage bands. These descriptors are a starting template — tap any one to tailor the wording to your goal, so it becomes the shared language you'll use to place and grow students.

Well Above Typical
Student independently and consistently demonstrates this skill with a high level of accuracy and efficiency, flexibly applying strategies to complex or unfamiliar tasks and communicating their reasoning with precise academic vocabulary and detailed explanations.
Above Typical
Student accurately demonstrates this skill using appropriate strategies, explains their reasoning clearly, shows strong conceptual understanding, and applies it independently in most grade-level situations.
Typical
Student demonstrates this skill on grade-level tasks with occasional teacher support and can explain their thinking, though accuracy or consistency may vary.
Below Typical
Student demonstrates partial understanding and requires frequent support; may struggle to select effective strategies, use models, or clearly explain their reasoning.
Well Below Typical
Student demonstrates limited understanding and cannot yet complete grade-level tasks consistently, even with significant support, and has difficulty explaining their thinking.
Your percentage bands and descriptors become the shared scale for this SLO. You'll finalize and lock everything after you build your Personal Plan — so you can still adjust the wording and cut points until then.
